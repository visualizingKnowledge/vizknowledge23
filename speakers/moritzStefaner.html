Moritz Stefaner is the operator of Truth & Beauty and writer/co-host of the Data Stories podcast. With a background in cognitive science and interface design, his work strives to find meaningful insights and beauty in large and complex data sets. He balances an eye for aesthetics with a sensitivity for mapping complex phenomena to support data–driven decision making.
